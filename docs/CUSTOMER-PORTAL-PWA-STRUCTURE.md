# Hail Mary Customer Portal PWA - Full Structure

## Executive Summary

The Customer Portal is a personalized PWA that replaces traditional PDF quotes and generic brochures with an **interactive, dynamic, future-proof home energy experience**. Accessible via a unique magic link, customers receive their own digital twin of their home's energy journey.

---

## Access & Authentication

### Magic Link System
```
https://portal.hailmary.uk/{CUSTOMER_TOKEN}
Example: https://portal.hailmary.uk/AB3X9-JONES
```

- **No password required** - secure magic token authentication
- **Cross-platform** - works on phone, tablet, and desktop
- **"Add to Home Screen"** prompt for native app experience
- **Offline-capable** - full functionality after first load
- **Auto-updates** - syncs when spec or roadmap changes
